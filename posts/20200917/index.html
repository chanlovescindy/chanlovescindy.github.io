想起了《傲慢与偏见》

今天想起了《傲慢与偏见》我印象最深的一段：

女主：”But I was embarrassed.“

男主：“And so was I.”

女主：“You might have talked to me more when you came to dinner”

男主：“A man who had felt less, might.”

你总是嫌我不会找话题，不会安慰人。”A man who had felt less, might“。
